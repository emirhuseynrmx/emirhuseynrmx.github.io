05 — FAQ

Questions, answered.

The things people usually ask before we start.

What do you actually build?
Auditable, high-performance systems for AI, fintech, and data — decision engines, cost-governance layers, pre-trade risk engines, and ML pipelines. The common thread: every decision the system makes can be explained, replayed, and verified.
What does "delivered with proof" mean?
Every delivery ships with a verifiable acceptance report: it builds, tests pass, it's documented, and another team can run it — checked by Calybris Core and reproducible by anyone, not just taken on trust.
Are your systems open source?
The core is: Calybris Core, the deterministic decision kernel, is published on crates.io under Apache-2.0. Products built on top of it (Goveris, Aegis, and others) are separate.
What technologies do you work with?
Rust for high-performance, safety-critical cores; Python for ML, causal inference, and data pipelines; plus the surrounding stack — FastAPI, PyData, and reproducible, tested delivery with CI.
How can I work with you?
Reach out by email or on Upwork with what you need to de-risk. I take on consulting, system-architecture reviews, and building proof-carrying AI, fintech, and data systems.
