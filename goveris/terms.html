Pilot terms

Observe first. Enforce only after approval.

The standard GOVERIS pilot is a metadata-only, observe-only assessment. It does not alter production model calls. Provider proxying or enforcement requires a separate written scope and technical acceptance.

Deliverables

The agreed package may include an executive PDF, local dashboard, aggregate findings, review queue, what-if policy scenarios, and evidence hashes. Package limits are those shown in the signed order form.

Claims

Savings figures are catalog-based scenarios until validated against customer traffic and outcomes. GOVERIS does not guarantee a savings amount, model quality, or business result.

Commercial and legal boundary

Website pricing is an indicative product scope, not a binding offer. Taxes, cloud/provider charges, custom adapters, service levels, liability limits, and data-processing obligations belong in the signed customer agreement. This page is an engineering template, not legal advice.
