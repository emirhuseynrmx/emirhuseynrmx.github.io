03 — Signal Audit

Kill Overfitted Strategies

MDL-inspired complexity penalties. More complex signals need exponentially stronger evidence.

Signal Audit Pipeline
Signal Ingest → MDL Complexity Score → Walk-Forward (5-fold) → Cost Deduction → Leakage Detection → Regime Robustness → Evidence Grade
Proof digest: 2e4d65ed22008e05b3a1c4f7...89d2 (SHA-256, deterministic)

MDL Penalty

log2(params) * 200 + sqrt(lookback) * 100 + indicators * 150 + optimization * 1000 + snooping^2 * 300

Walk-Forward

5-fold expanding window. In-sample 70%, out-of-sample 30%. Must profit in 3+ folds after costs.

Leakage Detection

First 5% returns vs rest. Suspiciously high early returns flag look-ahead bias. Snooping degree tracked.

Regime Robustness

Split into 3 regimes. Must be profitable in at least 2/3. Pure bull-market signals fail.
