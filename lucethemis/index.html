03 Theory

MDL-Inspired Approach

Complexity & Evidence

Beyond Kolmogorov

Kolmogorov complexity and Solomonoff induction are not operational metrics. LuceThemis uses a practical MDL-inspired proxy: more complex signals must carry stronger evidence to pass the audit gate. This creates a natural Occam pressure — simple signals with consistent evidence pass; overfit complex patterns with thin evidence fail.
