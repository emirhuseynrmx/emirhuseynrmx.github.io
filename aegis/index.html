01 — THE PROBLEM

A churn score is not a decision.

Telecoms drown in dashboards that flag at-risk customers, but a probability doesn't tell an operator what to do, which customers are worth saving, or what a retention offer is actually worth. Prediction without causality is just an alarm nobody can act on.

02 — HOW IT WORKS

Predict → explain → prescribe.

01 · TAHMİN

Score the risk

A calibrated model ranks every customer by churn probability — the starting point, not the answer.

02 · AÇIKLA

Counterfactual why

For each at-risk customer, Aegis computes the counterfactual: the smallest change that would have kept them.

03 · YÖNLENDİR

Action + revenue

A causal treatment recommendation ranked by expected revenue saved — a decision, not a dashboard.
